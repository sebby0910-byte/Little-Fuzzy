Chapter 2

One evening, Jack finally meets the source of the mysterious tracks: small, furry creatures, no taller than children, with curious eyes and nimble hands.

Intelligent and sociable, they quickly earn his trust. Jack welcomes them into his cabin, offering food and shelter, and soon names them “Fuzzies.” Their playful spirit and quick learning reveal that they are far more than ordinary animals.

From this first meeting begins a bond of trust and wonder—one that will challenge humanity’s view of life on Zarathustra forever.
