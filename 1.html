Chapter 1

Jack Holloway, an old independent prospector on Zarathustra, lives alone in a cabin deep in the planet’s forests. One evening, while returning home, he encounters small, furry creatures unlike anything he has ever seen before.

Curious, intelligent, and unafraid, the creatures quickly form a bond with Jack. He names them “Fuzzies” and offers them food and shelter. Their playful nature and surprising cleverness make him wonder if they are more than just animals.

This meeting sparks a question that will shape the fate of Zarathustra: are the Fuzzies simply another native species, or are they truly sentient beings?
